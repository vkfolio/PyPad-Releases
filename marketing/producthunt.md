## 💬 FAQs (Add to PH page)

**Q: Is PyPad free?**
A: Yes, completely free and open source.

**Q: Does it include Python?**
A: No — it uses whatever Python interpreter you have installed (and auto-discovers them). You can also add custom interpreter paths.

**Q: Can I use it for interview prep?**
A: Absolutely. 90+ DSA templates, live visualizer, debugger — it's built for that.

**Q: Is there a Linux version?**
A: Not yet, but the codebase is cross-platform. Contributions welcome!

**Q: How is this different from VS Code?**
A: PyPad is a scratchpad, not a full IDE. Zero-config, instant run, built-in algorithm visualizer, and focused on quick experimentation rather than project management.
